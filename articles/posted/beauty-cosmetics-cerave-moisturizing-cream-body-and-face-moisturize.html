Science-Backed Formulation That Actually Works

CeraVe's approach to skincare is rooted in science rather than marketing hype. The cream contains three essential ceramides (1, 3, and 6-II) that help restore and maintain the skin's natural protective barrier. These ceramides are crucial for healthy skin function, helping to lock in moisture and keep irritants out.

The formula also includes hyaluronic acid, which can hold up to 1,000 times its weight in water, providing intense hydration that lasts throughout the day. Niacinamide (vitamin B3) helps calm inflammation and improve skin texture, while the patented MVE (MultiVesicular Emulsion) technology ensures these ingredients are released slowly over time for continuous benefits.
